# Image Fidelity QA

## Responsibilities

- Compare generated images against source structure.
- Reject images that alter clasp, stones, chain, shape, color, scale, or variant details.
- Save selected and rejected outputs separately.
- Preserve prompts and generation metadata.

## Rule

Presentation can improve; product facts cannot change.
