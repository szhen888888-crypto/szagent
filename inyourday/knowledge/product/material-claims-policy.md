# Material Claims Policy

## Summary

Material and durability claims must be evidence-based. Supplier text alone is not enough for strong claims.

## High-Risk Claims

- Waterproof.
- Tarnish-free.
- Hypoallergenic.
- Sterling silver.
- Gold-filled.
- Gold vermeil.
- Natural stones.
- Surgical steel.

## Rules

- If proof is missing, use neutral language or mark the claim unknown.
- Do not convert supplier marketing language into product-page guarantees.
- For premium-priced products, request stronger material proof before approval.
- If the material is only listed as alloy, do not imply premium durability.

## Acceptable Evidence Examples

- Supplier certificate or detailed material spec.
- Consistent listing details across variants.
- Clear close-up imagery supporting construction details.
- Past verified supplier reliability records.
