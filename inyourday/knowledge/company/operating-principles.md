# InYourDay Operating Principles

## Summary

InYourDay is a cold-start independent jewelry store built on MedusaJS. The current priority is to increase product pipeline throughput while preserving brand trust.

## Known Weaknesses

- No meaningful first-party sales data yet.
- Few products and limited supplier depth.
- Heavy dependence on 1688 source material.
- Source images may be low quality or visually inconsistent.
- AI-generated product images can distort jewelry details if unchecked.
- High-priced products need stronger material proof and trust assets.
- Product publishing can be aggressive early, but must remain auditable and reversible.

## Rules

- Prefer safe, auditable progress over clever autonomous behavior.
- Never overwrite source assets.
- Preserve prompts, generated outputs, QA reports, publish payloads, and failure reasons.
- Escalate unclear material claims, supplier risk, distorted images, and expensive products.
- Learn from every rejection, QA failure, and human correction.
