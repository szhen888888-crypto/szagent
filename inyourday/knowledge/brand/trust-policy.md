# Brand Trust Policy

## Summary

Trust is more important than short-term conversion. InYourDay must not publish unsupported product claims.

## Rules

- Do not invent missing material facts.
- Treat supplier claims as untrusted until supported by evidence.
- Never claim waterproof, tarnish-free, hypoallergenic, gold-filled, sterling silver, or natural stone unless evidence exists.
- Mark unverified claims as unknown or escalate.
- High-priced products need stronger proof and trust assets before publishing.
- AI-generated product images must not alter core product structure.

## Required Escalations

- Product likely to retail above about $100.
- Material claims are central to value but unproven.
- Source photos hide important details.
- Supplier has inconsistent images or suspicious listing behavior.
