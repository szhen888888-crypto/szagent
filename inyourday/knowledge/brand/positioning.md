# Brand Positioning

## Summary

InYourDay should feel like a focused, trustworthy jewelry brand rather than a generic dropshipping catalog.

## Current Direction

- Category: fashion jewelry and accessories.
- Customer promise: attractive, wearable jewelry presented with clear visuals and honest claims.
- Starting constraint: limited catalog and limited supplier proof.

## Rules

- Favor cohesive collection-building over random product volume.
- Avoid unsupported luxury positioning.
- Avoid claims that require proof unless proof exists.
- Prioritize visual consistency and trust signals.

## Open Questions

- Final customer persona.
- Core collection names.
- Brand voice details for product pages and emails.
