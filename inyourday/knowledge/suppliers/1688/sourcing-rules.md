# 1688 Sourcing Rules

## Summary

1688 is the main early sourcing channel, but source listings require verification before entering the product pipeline.

## Rules

- Capture source URL, title, price, MOQ, supplier name, material claims, and source images.
- Prefer listings with clear variant images and detail shots.
- Treat supplier marketing claims as untrusted until supported.
- Avoid items whose source images hide key construction details.
- Favor products that can fill a collection or SEO story.
- Reject items that look generic, cheap, or difficult to represent faithfully.

## Supplier Risk Signals

- Inconsistent product images across variants.
- Missing detail shots.
- Suspiciously strong material claims with no proof.
- Poor shop consistency.
- Product appears copied from another brand's imagery.
